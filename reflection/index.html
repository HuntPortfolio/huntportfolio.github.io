Step by Step Guide to Better Technical Writing:

  1. Understand your audience. Tone and formality should be a reflection of the intended audience.
  2. Information should be delivered factually, never as conjecture or inference.
  3. Sources are key, more importantly dversify sources and search for trends.
  4. Always make sure to engage the reader.
  5. Be consice, eliminate any unnecessary words or phrases.
  6. Proofread, grammar and spelling mistakes are distracting.
