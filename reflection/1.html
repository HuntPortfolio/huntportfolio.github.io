Criteria 1

Student Learning Goal 1: Students write both to learn and to communicate what they learn.


Not completing this goal is almost impossible, however I searched for instances of "fluff" and passive voice.

For fluff, I used BlahBlah Meter to analyze all the polished works. The site scans the text for empty words and awkward grammer and returns a number between 0 and 1 (full explanation).

I found that passive voice almost always correlated to an awkward or weakly-worded sentence. Using passive voice isn't necessarily bad, however this graph visualizes how often it's used.
